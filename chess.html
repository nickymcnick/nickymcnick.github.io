Chess Concept Design

This chess set is an exploration of opposites. During my research of other chess set designs I kept coming back to those who had very different and opposing pieces for each side.I began to brainstorm different opposites from very abstract ideas such as light vs. dark, to more physical representations like straight lines vs. curved lines. The final concept at its most basic representation is a depiction of constructed structures, almost building-like vs. organic and natural forms, some even imitating human or animal forms.
