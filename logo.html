Personal Branding

The search for a personal logo has been frustrating. Towards the end I decided to use my initials of N and R. As with most of my projects at first I research what other design solutions people before me have come up with, taking note of the elements of design that worked and I liked. Then I proceeded to take those elements and try them out on my own exploring and pushing until I got somewhere I liked what I saw or hit a wall. Once the concept on paper was very clear I transferred it to the digital realm where I continued to tweak it until I was satisfied with the width, the angle and the curvature of the lines.

  • The final product is a simple and elegant solution that plays with the mind and imagination by making the viewer complete the letters. It can also be both bold and delicate depending on the application, making it a versatile logo.
