Solstice Interactive

Solstice Interactive is a startup focusing on bringing solar energy for urban areas or to people unable to put solar panels on their property. This design concept came from an idea hack where part of the challenge is creating an engaging app. Through the visualization of energy data the app allows the user to see their positive impact on the environment as an individual and as a member of a community. The app also has a gamification element that allows the user to be further engaged with Solstice Interactive. The app itself can be a very basic view of the energy and money saved. However, here you can see how much further the app can go and potentially further engage the user.

  • The first few panels show the simple interface which depicts in various visual ways the amount of energy and money saved by the customer. The user can easily also see the impact of their community of Solstice Interactive customers, and even the whole state of Massachusetts.

  • The gamification comes into play with the growing trees which as the customer saves more energy he or she will see the trees grow and join a forest on the bottom of the screen. As the forest grows the user can earn badges which they can then share with other fellow Solstice Interactive customers or their friends on social media.
